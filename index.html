Lab 3 Mission

In a galaxy not so far away, a young cybersecurity padawan embarks on a mission to protect sensitive information from the ever-watching eyes of the search engine empire. With the power of robots.txt and reCAPTCHA, they defend the secrets of the Rebel Alliance... one HTTP header at a time.

Some confidential sections of this site are hidden from search engines and not directly accessible. Only those who know the path may proceed.
